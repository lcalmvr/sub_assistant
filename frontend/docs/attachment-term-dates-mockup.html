Design Notes

  • Attachment-Anchored: Dates are assigned to attachment points (e.g., $5M, $10M), not carrier names
  • Primary Auto-Assignment: Earliest date automatically assigns to attachment $0 (primary layer)
  • Carrier Helper: Shows which carrier is currently at each attachment point for context
  • TBD Support: Dates can be marked as TBD (common for mid-term quoting)
  • Persistent Dates: When carriers change, dates stay with their attachment points
  • Multi-Date Warning: System warns when multiple dates are configured
  • Centralized Management: All date edits happen in Policy Terms section, tower grid just displays
  • Sharing: Dates can be easily shared across excess options using same attachment points
